My friend and I now frequently bike around our neighborhood, usually by the ocean, or we will go to downtown Brooklyn or Manhattan. I've gotten a lot more comfortable with biking recently, and has allowed me to discover the city in a whole new way.

As of today I have close to 300 hours volunteering, and I find it to be a personally very rewarding activity, as it has a tangible feasible impact, and even if I'm only a small part of that, I am still fulfilled.
