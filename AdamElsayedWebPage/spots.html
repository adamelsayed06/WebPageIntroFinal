NYC -- Must-Go Spots

(and some you must avoid)

Back to Home

The Statue of Liberty

A classic for a reason. The historical nuance surrounding this statue, along with the iconic nature of it make it a must see for any tourist. You don't have to visit it more than once or twice, but that very first time is magical. Verdict: Go See It

Joe's Pizza

Although Joe's Pizza is a solid spot for pizza, it is not the best place to get Pizza in NYC. If you want an experience more close to what a local would get, I recommend getting dollar slice pizza or Vesuvios. Joe's is overrated and overpriced for the quality of the slice.
Verdict: Save Your Time & Money

Viewing Deck of NYC Skyline

NYC is absolutely FAMED for it's skyline, and I think the best way to experience it is through a viewing deck. Although during midday they can get extremely crowded, I recommend either going very early or very late. Although it will be busy late at night, the views of the glowing city is best at night.
Verdict: Go See It
