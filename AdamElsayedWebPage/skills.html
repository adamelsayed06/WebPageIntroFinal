Philosophy has been a topic that I found myself very interested in for a number of reasons . I found being introspective, and examining my thought process, why I thought the way I did, whether that be on how we attain knowledge, or my ethical framework was very interesting. However, there is a lot of fluff, and books that are either incomprehensible or I just don't think are worth reading. If you are looking to get into philosophy here are the books I recommend for you to begin with.



Ethics 101 A great introduction to basic thought experiments and different moral frameworks. Knowledge, Reality, and Value© by Michael Huemer A general common sense guide to philosophy that most people will resonate with. A classic contemporary masterpiece. Meditations A timeless classic that influences the thoughts of most great Roman empires, a Must-Read.
